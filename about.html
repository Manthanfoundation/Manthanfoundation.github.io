About Manthan

Manthan Foundation Charitable Trust

Manthan Foundation Charitable Trust (NGO Manthan) is well established NGO which is providing free education to children with different disabilities like Cerebral Palsy, Intellectual disability, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Down Syndrome, Locomotor Disability etc. at Manthan Special School. We also provide Free Rehabilitation to children with special need through physiotherapy, occupational therapy, Speech Therapy, Counselling, Assistive Devices at Manthan Rehab Centre. Manthan also provide Education and Rehabilitation in slum and rural area where people are not aware about disabilities and its management through CBR Centres. And Manthan Art Gallery where vocational training are given to those children who have completed their primary education and ready to earn money.
