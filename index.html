About NGO Manthan

Manthan Foundation Charitable Trust (NGO Manthan) is well established NGO which is providing free education to children with different disabilities like Cerebral Palsy, Intellectual disability, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Down Syndrome, Locomotor Disability etc. at Manthan Special School. We also provide Free Rehabilitation to children with special need through physiotherapy, occupational therapy, Speech Therapy, Counselling, Assistive Devices at Manthan Rehab Centre. Manthan also provide Education and Rehabilitation in slum and rural area where people are not aware about disabilities and its management through CBR Centres. And Manthan Art Gallery where vocational training are given to those children who have completed their primary education and ready to earn money.

At present we has 50 children from age of one year onwards

We’re Helping Hand On 50+ Children In Alwar District

MSS - Manthan Special School

The Manthan Special School, popularly known as MSS, is a special school for children with Intellectual disability, Cerebral Palsy and multiple disability. Where we provide special education to these children.

MCBR - Manthan Community Based Rehabilitation Centre

Some profoundly retarded children are also available in our society who are not able to reach our unit. For these children we focus on training them in performing partially the basic skills through our CBR centres.

MDRC - Manthan Divyang Rehabilitation Centre

MDRC - Therapy Unit for Disabled using a multi therapy approach. By offering the services of physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, Special education, parent counselling all under one roof.
